Episode 47 - Death Cloud Particle
=================================

Problem:
 - A single death cloud particle when a creature dies.

Solution:
 - Given an angle, center, and a speed, flies outward from the center
 - single loop animation, expires when finished
 - Animation:
     "NpcSym"
     x=1,y=0,
     w=1,h=1,
     fps=18, num_frames=7
